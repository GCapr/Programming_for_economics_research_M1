Who Is This For?

This course is designed for:

  • Economics PhD students learning empirical methods
  • Quantitative social scientists (political science, sociology, public policy)
  • Research assistants working with data
  • Industry researchers transitioning from academia
  • Anyone with a quantitative background who wants to learn modern research tools

Prerequisites

This course assumes:

  • Basic familiarity with statistics (mean, variance, regression)

  • Willingness to learn and make mistakes!
